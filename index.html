Clinically validated

Self-Assessment Tools

Standardised screening tools used by healthcare professionals worldwide. For self-awareness only — not a clinical diagnosis.

PHQ-9 — Patient Health Questionnaire

The PHQ-9 is a 9-question tool that screens for the presence and severity of depressive symptoms. It is widely used by GPs and mental health clinicians. Please rate how often you have been bothered by the following over the last 2 weeks.

PHQ-9 Questionnaire

Over the last 2 weeks, how often have you been bothered by any of the following problems?

Not at all (0)
Several days (1)
More than half (2)
Nearly every day (3)
Answered: 0 / 9

GAD-7 — Generalised Anxiety Disorder Scale

The GAD-7 is a 7-question screening tool for generalised anxiety disorder. It assesses anxiety symptoms and their impact on functioning. Please rate how often you have been bothered by the following over the last 2 weeks.

GAD-7 Questionnaire

Over the last 2 weeks, how often have you been bothered by any of the following problems?

Not at all (0)
Several days (1)
More than half (2)
Nearly every day (3)
Answered: 0 / 7
